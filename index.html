About Us

Haridwar University, Roorkee formerly known as Roorkee College of Engineering is awarded the “Best Upcoming Engineering College in Uttarakhand Award” under CMAI National Uttarakhand Award Jointly by CMAI, AICTE, UTU and awarded the “Best Technical Institute in Uttarakhand Award” by CMAI, AICTE, and MTU. Haridwar University offers undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma programs in the discipline of Engineering, Pharmacy, Management, Tourism Management, etc.
